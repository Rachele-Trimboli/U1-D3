Focaccia with Caramelized Onions, Cranberries, and Brie

Wow your taste buds with this festive appetizer topped with sweet caramelized onions, tart crambies, and creamy bie. It will become your new favorite holiday starter!

Image: Sally Vegas

Fresh Cranberry Recipies That Go Beyond Sauce

Ingredients

For the Focaccia

  • 4 cups(500g) all purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 taspons instant yeast
  • 1 1/2 teaspons salt
  • 1 3/4 cups (350g) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil, plus more for the pan

For the Topping

  • 3 medium yellow onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoon olive oil, for brushing on top
  • 10 ounces brie, cut into cubes (scrape rind off with a paring knife if desired, though it's not necessary)
  • 1 1/2 cups fresh or frozen cranberries

Method

  1. Make the focaccia dough

    2. In a medium sized mixing bowl, or in a stand mixer with a paddle attachment, mix togheter all the dough ingredients until the flour is fully absorbed. You don't need actually knead the dough, though I sometimes knead it a few times in the bowl when mixing by hand to incorporate the last of the flour...
